    • About Our Food Tour

    A Taste of the Highlands

    We are a local-inspired food tour experience dedicated to showcasing the flavors, culture, and everyday life of La Trinidad, Benguet. Our goal is to connect visitors with the people, places, and foods that make this highland town special. We believe that food is one of the best ways to understand a place. That is why our tour focuses not only on what you eat, but also on where it comes from and the stories behind it.

    Why We Created This Tour?

    How can people experience La Trinidad in a more meaningful way?

    Many visitors come for strawberries, but La Trinidad has more to offer. It has vegetable farms, local markets, family-run food stalls, and a culture deeply connected to the land.

    We wanted to create a food tour that celebrates all of this in one experience. Our tour was designed to help guests slow down, taste fresh highland produce, and discover the everyday beauty of Benguet through food.

    A Town of Fresh Harvests

    La Trinidad is known for its strawberry farms and fresh vegetables, making it one of the most recognized food destinations in Benguet. But beyond the popular attractions, the town has a deeper identity rooted in farming, local tradition, and community life.
